Get Your Fix

From fan fiction to LitRPG, from hungry vampires to angsty poets, from tongue-tied aliens to articulate dragons, encounter everything imagination can offer. And if there’s a book you want to read, but it hasn’t been written yet, then write it!

Whether you’re a budding scribbler seeking to test the waters or an established novelist experimenting with a new concept, add your voice to storytelling’s newest evolution. Build a following of engaged readers who want you to succeed and can’t wait for your next chapter.

Goodbye Gatekeepers

Forget finding a literary agent, begging an intern at a publishing house to read your work, and earning a puny royalty at the end of the day. Forget self-publishing and hoping your friends buy your book. No one gets to decide the worth of your work, but you. At Bibbit, nothing holds you back from your potential readers.

Turn Passion into a Living

We know how hard it is to write for a living. Link your profile to Patreon and enable your readers to support you. Some writers earn thousands of dollars a month this way. Bibbit and your soon-to-be readers want you realize your passion.
